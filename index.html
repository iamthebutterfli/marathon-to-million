About Malcolm Jenkins

📸 Malcolm's Photo

Founder & Visionary

Two-time Super Bowl Champion Malcolm Jenkins has dedicated his life to creating opportunity both on and off the field. As a 13-year NFL veteran, CNN contributor, and social justice advocate, Malcolm brings the same leadership and determination that defined his athletic career to his philanthropic mission.

Founded in 2010, the Malcolm Jenkins Foundation represents Malcolm's commitment to youth development, education equity, and economic empowerment in underserved communities. His hands-on approach and personal investment in every program ensures authentic, lasting impact.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible? ▼
Yes! The Malcolm Jenkins Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Your donation is tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law. You'll receive a receipt for your records immediately after donating.
How is my donation used? ▼
87% of every dollar goes directly to our programs. The remaining 13% covers essential operational costs to ensure we can continue delivering impact. We maintain transparency through annual reports and regular updates to our donors.
Can I make a gift of stock or cryptocurrency? ▼
Absolutely! We accept gifts of appreciated stock, cryptocurrency, and other assets. These gifts often provide additional tax benefits. Contact us at donate@malcolmjenkinsfoundation.org for transfer instructions.
Does my employer match charitable gifts? ▼
Many employers offer matching gift programs that can double or triple your impact! Check with your HR department or use our employer matching tool. We'll handle all the paperwork to make it easy.
Can I set up a recurring monthly donation? ▼
Yes! Join our MVP Circle by setting up a monthly gift. You can modify or cancel anytime, and you'll receive special updates showing your ongoing impact throughout the year.
